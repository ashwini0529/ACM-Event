ABOUT THIS EVENT

Welcome to CodeBurst . Ready, Steady, Code.!

The event is a blend of technical and fun both. It is divided in two rounds. The first one being technical comprising of three parts namely competitive coding, debugging and reverse coding which would be executed online. The second round brings fun with technical at background.

The fun being bursting the balloons enclosing question with darts. If the participants fail to burst the balloons they are an alternative to questions from a question bank by throwing dices.
